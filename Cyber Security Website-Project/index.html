What is Cybersecurity?

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.

Why It Matters

From personal identity theft to national security risks, cybersecurity affects us all.

Digital Defense

Encryption, firewalls, and multi-factor authentication help keep data safe.

The Pillars of Cybersecurity

  • Confidentiality: Your data should only be seen by the right people.
  • Integrity: Data must remain accurate and trustworthy.
  • Availability: Information must be accessible when needed.

Threats You Should Know

The digital world is full of cyber threats, including:

  • Phishing: Fraudulent emails tricking users into giving personal data.
  • Malware: Viruses and ransomware infecting devices.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating people to gain confidential access.
  • Data Breaches: Hackers stealing personal and financial data.

How to Stay Secure

Follow these basic cybersecurity tips:

  • Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
  • Keep software and devices updated.
  • Beware of suspicious emails and messages.
  • Backup important data regularly.
